# Document Checklist — Canadian Tax Preparation

Use this checklist based on the taxpayer's profile. Check off what's been provided;
request what's missing in a single consolidated message.

---

## Universal (Everyone)

- [ ] **SIN** — Do NOT store in workbook; confirm the user has it for their own filing
- [ ] **Prior year Notice of Assessment (NOA)** — Shows RRSP room, any carryforwards, prior year net income
- [ ] **CRA My Account printout** — Confirms registered slips received, RRSP room, benefit history
- [ ] **Government-issued slips** received from CRA's My Account or mailed (T4, T5, T3, T4A, etc.)

---

## T4 Employment Income

- [ ] **T4 slip** from each employer
- [ ] **Year-end pay stub** — confirms YTD totals if T4 not yet available
- [ ] **T4 Box 38/39** — If employee received RSUs or stock options, note the amount
- [ ] **T2200** — If claiming employment expenses (e.g., home office as employee)

---

## Self-Employment Income (T2125)

- [ ] **Business bank statements** — All months, showing client deposits
- [ ] **Business credit card statements** — All months
- [ ] **Invoices to clients** — Or summary of invoices by client
- [ ] **Payment confirmations** — E.g., EFT remittances, wire receipts, PayPal/Stripe summaries
- [ ] **USD income documentation** — Invoices in USD with conversion rates or Wise/bank FX records
- [ ] **HST/GST collected** — Note whether amounts include or exclude HST
- [ ] **HST/GST remitted** — Any net HST remittances reduce effective business income

---

## Business Expenses

- [ ] **Receipts folder** — All expense receipts (PDFs, images)
- [ ] **Software/subscription records** — Annual or monthly charges
- [ ] **Professional development** — Course receipts, conference registrations
- [ ] **Professional fees paid** — Accountant, lawyer, consultant invoices
- [ ] **Home internet & phone bills** — At least 1 month; note business-use %
- [ ] **Business insurance policy** — Annual premium
- [ ] **Office supply receipts** — Anything purchased for the business
- [ ] **Mileage log** — If claiming vehicle expenses

---

## Home Office (Detailed Method)

- [ ] **Property square footage** — Total home and office space (or floor plan)
- [ ] **Mortgage statement** — Annual interest paid (interest only, not principal)
- [ ] **HELOC statement** — If any HELOC interest relates to the property
- [ ] **Property tax bill** — Annual amount from municipality
- [ ] **Home insurance policy** — Annual premium
- [ ] **Utility bills** — Hydro, gas, water — annual totals or monthly average
- [ ] **Home maintenance receipts** — Repairs only (not renovations)
- [ ] **Condo fees** — If applicable

---

## RRSP

- [ ] **RRSP contribution receipts** — Official receipts from the financial institution
  - Include first-60-days-of-next-year contributions (Jan 1 – ~Mar 1)
  - Separate receipts for: own RRSP vs. spousal RRSP contributions
- [ ] **RRSP year-end statement** — Account balance as of Dec 31
- [ ] **T4RSP** — If any RRSP withdrawals made
- [ ] **Prior year NOA** — Shows available RRSP room
- [ ] **HBP repayment schedule** — If participating in Home Buyers' Plan

---

## TFSA

- [ ] **TFSA year-end statement** — Balance and transaction history
- [ ] **TFSA contribution tracking** — Total contributions vs. available room
  - Room = $7,000 (2025) + prior-year unused room + prior-year withdrawals
  - Over-contribution: 1% per month penalty

---

## RESP

- [ ] **RESP contribution confirmation** — Annual contributions
- [ ] **CESG grant confirmation** — Government grant received
- [ ] **T4A(P)** or other RESP withdrawal slips — If child withdrew funds
- [ ] **Transfer documentation** — If RESP was transferred between institutions

---

## Investments & Capital Gains

- [ ] **Year-end brokerage statements** — All accounts (registered and non-registered)
- [ ] **T5008 slips** — Security dispositions (for tax purposes)
- [ ] **T3 slips** — Trust/fund income (ETF distributions, mutual funds)
- [ ] **T5 slips** — Dividend and interest income
- [ ] **ACB records** — Adjusted cost base for each security (some brokers provide this)
- [ ] **RSU/stock option documentation** — Vest date, fair market value at vest, shares sold
- [ ] **Morgan Stanley / employer stock plan statements** — If RSUs from employer

---

## Other Income & Credits

- [ ] **T4E** — Employment insurance benefits
- [ ] **T4A(OAS)** — Old age security (if 65+)
- [ ] **T4A(P)** — CPP/QPP benefits
- [ ] **Rental income documentation** — Revenue and expenses for each rental property
- [ ] **T4A** — Self-employment income, pension, withdrawals, scholarships
- [ ] **Foreign income slips** — From foreign employers or investments (convert to CAD)

---

## Deductions & Credits

- [ ] **Childcare receipts** — Daycare, nanny, after-school programs
  - Nanny: T4 must be issued; SIN required (CRA cross-checks)
- [ ] **Medical expense receipts** — Prescriptions, dental, vision, paramedical
  - Must exceed 3% of net income (or $2,759 for 2024, whichever is lower)
- [ ] **Charitable donation receipts** — Official receipts from registered charities only
- [ ] **Tuition slips (T2202)** — For post-secondary education
- [ ] **Student loan interest** — Form T1 Schedule 11
- [ ] **Professional/union dues** — T4 Box 44 or separate receipts

---

## Prior Year Items (Optional but Useful)

- [ ] **Prior year T1 return** — Confirms carryforwards, prior-year deductions
- [ ] **Capital loss carryforward** — From NOA or prior returns
- [ ] **RRSP unused room carryforward** — From NOA

---

## Not Required in Workbook (Remind User to Keep Separately)

- SIN numbers of family members
- Banking information for direct deposit (CRA My Account)
- Prior year access codes
