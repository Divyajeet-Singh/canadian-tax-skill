## Schedule 3 — Capital Gains

Realized gains/losses from:
- Publicly traded shares (T5008)
- ETFs and mutual funds (T3, T5008)
- Real estate (not principal residence)
- Crypto (treated as property — report all dispositions)
- Business investment losses (ABIL) — 50% deductible against any income

**ACB tracking:** For identical shares, use the weighted average ACB method across all
non-registered accounts. RRSP/TFSA dispositions are NOT reported on Schedule 3.

## RRSP Rules (Quick Reference)

- **Contribution deadline:** 60 days after Dec 31 (approx. Feb 28/Mar 1)
- **Contribution limit:** 18% of prior year earned income, max $31,560 (2024); $32,490 (2025)
- **Over-contribution buffer:** $2,000 lifetime buffer (no penalty if within buffer)
- **Over-contribution penalty:** 1% per month on excess above $2,000 buffer
- **RRSP room:** Shown on latest Notice of Assessment (NOA)
- **Spousal RRSP:** Contributions to spouse's RRSP are claimed by the contributor
- **HBP:** First home withdrawal up to $35,000; must repay over 15 years
- **LLP:** Lifelong learning plan; up to $10,000/year; max $20,000 total

## TFSA Rules (Quick Reference)

- Contributions are NOT deductible (after-tax dollars)
- Growth and withdrawals are TAX-FREE
- **2025 room:** $7,000/year (same as 2024)
- Withdrawals create equivalent new room the following year
- Over-contributions: 1% per month penalty on excess
- Foreign income inside TFSA may attract withholding tax from source country

## RESP Rules (Quick Reference)

- Contributions NOT deductible
- CESG (Canada Education Savings Grant): 20% on first $2,500/year = $500/year max; $7,200 lifetime
- RESP-to-RESP transfers: NOT a taxable event; no T4A issued
- When child withdraws (EAP): taxable in child's hands at their marginal rate
- Subscriber's principal returned tax-free
